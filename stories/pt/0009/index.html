Onde está o meu gato? Where is my cat? Wapi paka wangu?

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath

Translators without Borders, Rossana Lima, Priscilla Freitas de Oliveira

Alfredo Ferreira

Portuguese

Level 1

Onde está o meu gato?

Yuko wapi paka wangu?

Where is my cat?

Estará debaixo da cama?

Je, yuko chini ya kitanda?

Is it under the bed?

Estará em cima do armário?

Yuko juu ya kabati?

Is it on top of the cupboard?

Estará atrás do sofá?

Yuko nyuma ya sofa?

Is it behind the couch?

Estará ao lado da lixeira?

Yuko karibu na pipa la takataka?

Is it next to the bin?

Estará dentro da cesta?

Yuko ndani ya kikapu?

Is it inside the basket?

Estará fora de casa?

Yuko nje ya nyumba?

Is it outside the house?

Está aqui!

Yupo hapa!

Here it is!

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath
Translated by: Translators without Borders, Rossana Lima, Priscilla Freitas de Oliveira
Read by: Alfredo Ferreira
Language: Portuguese
Level 1
