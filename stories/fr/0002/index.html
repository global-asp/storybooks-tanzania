Regardez les animaux.

Look at the animals.

Tazama wanyama.

La vache dit, « Meuh. »

The cow says, "Moo."

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

La chèvre dit, « Bêê, bêê. »

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

Mbuzi anasema, "Mee, mee."

Le cheval dit, « Hiiii. »

The horse says, "Neigh."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

Le cochon dit, « Groin, groin. »

The pig says, "Grunt."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

La poule dit, « Cot-cot. »

The chicken says, "Cluck."

Kuku anasema, "Ko, ko, ko."

Le chien dit, « Wouf. »

The dog says, "Woof."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe, bwe."

Le fermier dit, « Chut ! »

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Mkulima anasema,"Nyamaza!"
