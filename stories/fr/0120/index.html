Cheveux Hair Nywele

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath

Alexandra Danahy

Alexandra Danahy

French

Level 1

Thuli a des cheveux courts.

Thuli has short hair.

Thuli ana nywele fupi.

Anna a des cheveux longs.

Anna has long hair.

Anna ana nywele ndefu.

Cathy a les cheveux les plus longs.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Cathy ana nywele ndefu kuliko wote.

Zama a tressé ses cheveux.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Zama amesuka nywele zake.

Baba a une barbe.

Baba has a beard.

Baba ana ndevu.

Zanele a peigné ses cheveux.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Zanele amechana nywele zake.

Thabo a coupé ses cheveux.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Thabo amenyoa nywele zake.

Themba a rasé ses cheveux.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Themba amenyoa kipara.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath
Translated by: Alexandra Danahy
Read by: Alexandra Danahy
Language: French
Level 1
