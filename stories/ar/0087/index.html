أُحِبُّ أَنْ أَقْرَأَ! Ninapenda kusoma! I like to read!

Letta Machoga

Wiehan de Jager

Hanadi Traifeh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk

Mashael Muhanna

Arabic

Level 1

أُحِبُّ أَنْ أَقْرَأَ.

Ninapenda kusoma.

I like to read.

لِمَنْ سَأقْرَأُ؟

Nimsomee nani?

Who can I read to?

أُخْتِي نَائِمَةٌ.

Mdogo wangu amelala.

My sister is asleep.

لِمَنْ سَأقْرَأُ؟

Nimsomee nani?

Who can I read to?

أُمِّي وَجَدَّتِي مَشْغُولَتَانِ.

Mama na bibi wana kazi.

My mother and grandmother are busy.

لِمَنْ سَأقْرَأ؟

Nimsomee nani?

Who can I read to?

أَبِي وَجَدِّي مَشْغُولَانِ.

Baba na babu wana kazi.

My father and grandfather are busy.

لِمَنْ سَأقْرَأ؟ سَأَقْرَأُ لِنَفْسِي!

Nimsomee nani? Nijisomee mwenyewe!

Who can I read to? I can read to myself!

Written by: Letta Machoga
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Hanadi Traifeh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk
Read by: Mashael Muhanna
Language: Arabic
Level: Level 1
Source: I like to read from African Storybook
