Engonya y'amakwa Enzalha The hungry crocodile Mamba mwenye njaa

Christian G.

Wiehan de Jager

Amos Mubunga Kambere

Lukhonzo

Level 1

Kera kera habya engonya eyikwire enzalha,

Hapo zamani, palikuwa na mamba mwenye njaa.

Once there was a very hungry crocodile.

Miyarondya ebyalya lyolholyolho kandi omwabukuthu. Neryo....

Alitafuta chakula taratibu na kwa ukimya. Halafu â€¦

He searched for food slowly and quietly. And then...

Mpwaaa!!! Engonya yawirira!

POO!!! Mamba anashambulia!

POW!!! The crocodile strikes!

Yebere yabiriwirira, muyamera, neryo yamiwutha. Neryo enzalha yamabalha neryo yamayanza

Baada ya hapo hana njaa tena, na anafurahi.

After that he is no longer hungry, and he is happy.

Kuhika ayakwira enzalha, kandi.

Hadi atakapopatwa na njaa tena.

Until he gets hungry again.

Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Amos Mubunga Kambere
